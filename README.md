# FUTURE_DS_03
📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis 

Excited to showcase my Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard project developed using Power BI. This project focuses on analyzing customer subscription behavior to identify the key factors influencing customer churn and retention.

🔍 Key Insights:
• Month-to-month contract customers showed the highest churn rate.
• Customers with lower tenure were more likely to leave the service.
 customer retention patterns.

💡 Business Recommendations:
• Encourage long-term subscriptions through loyalty programs and discounts.
• Strengthen onboarding and engagement initiatives for new customers.
• Improve service quality and pricing strategies for high-risk customer segments.

📈 Outcome:
This project provided valuable insights into customer retention challenges and demonstrated how data analytics can help organizations reduce churn, improve customer satisfaction, and support long-term business growth.
